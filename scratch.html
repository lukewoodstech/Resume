My Business: Woods Reptiles



Luke, a determined teenager with an unwavering passion for animals, particularly reptiles, found his calling in breeding boa constrictors and reticulated pythons. With an innate fascination for the unique beauty and behavior of these creatures, he embarked on a journey to turn his passion into a thriving business. Through meticulous research, hands-on experience, and a keen eye for genetics, Luke honed his skills and knowledge in the intricate art of reptile breeding. He learned the nuances of temperature regulation, habitat design, and genetic traits, transforming his bedroom into a miniature reptile sanctuary where he nurtured and observed his growing collection. Despite facing challenges and skeptics along the way, Luke's dedication and love for his craft propelled him forward, ultimately establishing a successful venture that not only provided him with financial independence but also enriched his understanding and appreciation of the natural world.





  1. Woods Reptiles (July 2019-July 2021)
    • Founded Woods Reptiles, a privately owned reptile breeding startup, focusing on producing high quality boa constrictors and reticulated pythons.
    • Developed and executed business plans, including market research, competitive analysis, and financial projections.
    • Grossed average $10,000 in yearly sales revenue over the course of business
    • Adapted quickly to challenges and pivoted business strategies as needed to capitalize on emerging opportunities and mitigate risks.
    • Demonstrated resilience, perseverance, and a growth mindset in navigating the complexities of entrepreneurship and overcoming obstacles.
